Desktop Support Technician (L1) • January 2024 - June 2025

Overview

Role snapshot

A Support Engineer is responsible for helping users or customers resolve technical issues related to software, hardware, or IT systems. The role sits between end-users and technical/development teams, ensuring systems run smoothly and problems are fixed quickly.

Focus Areas

Support Responsibilities

  • Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues (hardware, software, network, cloud services)
  • Respond to support tickets via email, chat, or phone
  • Monitor system performance and identify potential problems early
  • Document issues, solutions, and procedures in a knowledge base

Skills

Work Areas

  • IT Helpdesk / Service Desk (Level 1–2)
  • Cloud support (Azure, AWS, Microsoft 365)
  • Application or software support
  • Network or infrastructure support
